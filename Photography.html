  • Minor Photography

    Date: 03-02-2019
    Grade: 6.8

  • At The Hague University of Applied Sciences I followed the Minor Photography. For this I made different types of photos in different categories.

    The first theme I made pictures Texture & Rhytm. I photographed textures of objects as well as possible. For the rhytm part I had to take photos with repeating elements.

    The second theme is Styling. I took photos to advertise a bottle. The bottle was from a herbal liqueur of the brand Valhalla.

    The third Theme is Light. I photographed body parts using light to create beautiful shapes and contrasts.

    The fourth Theme is Time. Where you had to make pictures of an object that changes over time.

    The last Theme was a subject I could choose myself. I chose to make animal portraits, where the animal looks directly into the lens of the camera, making it look like a portrait of a certain animal.
