  • Improving the UX of Velvet Music

    Date: 01/07/2019

  • For the course "User Experience" at school, I made several projects with a project group related to User Experience. Three different design assignments and one theory assignment were made, in which me and my project group explained theory to the class.

    The design assignments were based upon the Velvet shop. Velvet is a shop that sells old-fashioned music in addition to the latest music. You can also choose to buy CDs and vinyl records instead of dowloading a track with Spotify.

    In this course he theory of User Experience was further discussed. Why do people do things the way they do? And for which reasons do they make choices? The aim here is to create a good interactive design with enough user experience.

  • Issue

    The Velvet Music online store allows the user to order a very wide range of music (and movies), but does not convey the shopping experience of the physical stores. The user experience of the Velvet online store is not optimal. As a result, Velvet cannot distinguish itself from their competitors in the field of online sales.

  • Objective

    The goal is to make the Velvet online website more enjoyable and meaningful for music lovers, existing customers and new customers. The starting point is the experience of the users through User Experience.

  • Design Report

    For this project I delivered several works with my project group, which is collected in a design report.

    The design report contains the design assignment of a benchmark and a storyboard, the design assignment of a detail page for Velvet and the design assignment of applying gamification on the Velvet website. It also contains the theory assignment by means of a presentation.
